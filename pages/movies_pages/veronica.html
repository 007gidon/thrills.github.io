Veronica

"Veronica" (Spanish: Verónica) is a gripping 2017 Spanish supernatural horror film directed by Paco Plaza. The movie features a compelling cast led by Sandra Escacena, Claudia Placer, Bruna González, Iván Chavero, and Ana Torrent. Plaza skillfully weaves a tale that is loosely based on true events from the chilling 1991 Vallecas case. The narrative unfolds around Veronica, portrayed by Sandra Escacena, who finds herself entangled in mysterious and supernatural occurrences. The film draws inspiration from the tragic real-life events surrounding Estefanía Gutiérrez Lázaro, who met an untimely and enigmatic demise after dabbling with a ouija board. Plaza's direction brings an eerie and atmospheric quality to the film, creating a sense of suspense that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. As the story explores the paranormal and supernatural elements, "Veronica" offers a visceral and unsettling cinematic experience. The film delves into the psychological and supernatural realms, blurring the lines between reality and the supernatural, leaving an indelible mark on the horror genre.
