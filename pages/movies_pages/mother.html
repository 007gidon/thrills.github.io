Mother

"Mother!" (2017) is an American fantasy drama written and directed by Darren Aronofsky, featuring a star-studded cast including Jennifer Lawrence, Javier Bardem, Ed Harris, Michelle Pfeiffer, Domhnall Gleeson, Brian Gleeson, and Kristen Wiig. The narrative centers around a young woman whose tranquil life with her husband at their country home is disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious couple. Premiering on September 5, 2017, at the 74th Venice International Film Festival, where it competed for the Golden Lion, the film was later released in the United States by Paramount Pictures on September 15, 2017. Garnering $44.5 million worldwide, "Mother!" received positive reviews for its intriguing storyline and compelling performances from the ensemble cast.
